Ball Clash Arena

Physics-based weapon balls clash in a neon arena. Bounce, aim, parry, block, clash weapons, combo abilities, and land cinematic supers.

Desktop: A/D or arrows move, W/Space/Up jump, mouse aim, left click attack, right click/Shift parry, Q/E abilities, R super. Mobile: use on-screen controls.

Tutorial

Use this as a quick public-test manual. The game is intentionally physics-first: movement bends momentum instead of replacing it.

1. Move and bounce

A/D or arrows steer. W/Space jumps. Your best control comes from pressing movement near wall/floor impacts to redirect the next bounce.

2. Aim and attack

Mouse or right touch pad aims. Left-click / Atk creates an active weapon hit with bonus damage and priority. Melee weapons also have passive collision damage.

3. Parry and block

Right-click or Shift. Perfect timing gives recoil, sparks, hitstop, super meter, and a punish window. Late block reduces damage. Bad timing leaves recovery.

4. Weapon clashes

Melee weapons, hands, staffs, spears, axes, hammers, and some projectiles can collide. Idle/passive weapons do not erase projectiles; active attacks, blocks, and perfect parries are required.

5. Abilities and supers

Q and E are character abilities. R spends a full super meter. Supers charge from time, movement, hits, parries, and combat pressure, but no longer instantly snowball.

6. Online rooms

Create or join a room, copy the invite link, choose 1v1 or Free For All, pick a fighter, then ready up. The server simulates online matches authoritatively; clients render snapshots and send inputs only.

7. Mobile controls

Left stick steers, right pad aims, buttons trigger Jump, Attack, Parry, Q, E, and R. Lower VFX and shake in Settings on older phones.

Credits / Controls

Ball Clash Arena is a self-contained HTML5 Canvas game with a small Node WebSocket room server.

Keyboard

Move: A/D or arrows. Jump: W/Space/Up. Attack: left mouse. Parry/block: right mouse or Shift. Q/E abilities. R super. Esc pause.

Game modes

Single PlayerMultiplayer 1v1Free For All

Hosting

Designed for GitHub + Render free/small services. No paid databases, asset CDNs, or native dependencies are required.

Known limitation

Online play is optimized for small friend groups. For best results, deploy Render in Frankfurt and keep players geographically close.

Multiplayer Lobby

Create a duel or free-for-all room, invite friends, choose fighters, and ready up. The server owns match state; clients send inputs and render authoritative snapshots.
